# Build Your Bot a Brain — Short Script v1 (60–90s)

## Hook (0:00–0:08)
Most people are using AI like a fancy chatbot.
That’s the bottleneck.
If you want real leverage, you need to build your bot a **brain**.

## Problem (0:08–0:20)
Chat-only bots forget context, lose decisions, and restart from zero every session.
So you keep repeating yourself, and nothing compounds.

## Shift (0:20–0:40)
A real AI brain has four parts:
1. **Memory** — what matters, persisted.
2. **Objectives** — what it’s trying to accomplish.
3. **Tools** — what it can actually do.
4. **Routines** — how it checks, learns, and improves.

## Demo Beat (0:40–1:05)
In Carapace + OpenClaw, we wire those pieces together:
- capture useful context,
- run tasks through the right model lane,
- trigger actions,
- and keep improving over time.

Now it’s not just answering prompts — it’s operating like a system.

## Close / CTA (1:05–1:20)
You don’t need a better prompt.
You need a better architecture.
Build your bot a brain.
